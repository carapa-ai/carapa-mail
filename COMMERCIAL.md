## FAQ

**Do I need a commercial license for personal use?**
No. The PolyForm Noncommercial license allows free use for any noncommercial purpose — personal, family, homelab, research, etc.

**Do I need a commercial license if I don't modify the code?**
The PolyForm Noncommercial license prohibits commercial use regardless of whether you modify the code. If your organization uses CarapaMail for business purposes, you need a commercial license.

**Can I try CarapaMail before buying?**
Yes. Install it, test it with your mail server, and buy the license when you're ready to deploy in production.

**What LLM providers are supported?**
Any provider with an OpenAI-compatible API: Anthropic Claude, local models via Ollama, llama.cpp, LM Studio, vLLM, Azure OpenAI, AWS Bedrock, etc. You bring your own API key.

**Is there a hosted/SaaS version?**
No. CarapaMail is self-hosted only. Your email stays on your infrastructure.
